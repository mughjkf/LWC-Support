💡 Frequently Asked Questions

1. I forgot my password. How can I reset it?

Currently, password resets are handled manually for security. Please contact us via email, and we will guide you through the process.

2. My score didn't update on the leaderboard. Why?

Leaderboards sync every few minutes. Please ensure you have a stable internet connection and try restarting the app.

3. I found a typo or an incorrect translation.

Please report the word and the language pair to our support email so we can correct it in the next update.

4. How do I delete my account?

To delete your account and all associated data, please send an email from your registered address with the subject "Account Deletion."
